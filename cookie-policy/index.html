What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by your browser at the request of a website. They contain identifiers that help a site recognise returning visitors and apply saved settings like language or contrast mode. Some cookies expire when you close the browser, while others persist for a defined period to improve continuity across visits. Related technologies include local storage, session storage, and pixels that operate with similar purposes. In this policy, we use “cookies” to refer to all such storage mechanisms unless noted otherwise. These mechanisms may support features tied to chicken road.
